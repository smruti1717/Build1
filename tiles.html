🏠 Ceramic Tiles
Application: Floors & walls (indoor)
Features: Easy maintenance, affordable
Approx. Price (per sq. ft): ₹30 – ₹60
Note: Commonly used for kitchens and bathrooms.


🧱 Vitrified Tiles
Application: Living rooms, commercial spaces
Features: Strong, low water absorption
Approx. Price (per sq. ft): ₹60 – ₹120
Note: Popular for durability and sleek finish.


🌿 Porcelain Tiles
Application: Indoors & outdoors
Features: Dense, stain & water resistant
Approx. Price (per sq. ft): ₹70 – ₹150
Note: Premium version of ceramic tiles.


✨ Marble Tiles
Application: Luxurious interiors
Features: Natural veining, elegant appearance
Approx. Price (per sq. ft): ₹100 – ₹250
Note: Needs sealing and maintenance.


🧼 Mosaic Tiles
Application: Bathrooms, pools, artistic walls
Features: Decorative, small size tiles
Approx. Price (per sq. ft): ₹40 – ₹80
Note: Often used for aesthetic patterns.


🔲 Cement Tiles
Application: Patios, vintage decor
Features: Handcrafted, colorful
Approx. Price (per sq. ft): ₹60 – ₹120
Note: Slightly porous, needs sealing.


🌞 Terracotta Tiles
Application: Outdoor areas, rustic floors
Features: Porous, earthy finish
Approx. Price (per sq. ft): ₹40 – ₹90
Note: Traditional look, absorbs moisture.
