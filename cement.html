🧱 Ordinary Portland Cement (OPC)
Grades: 33, 43, 53
Uses: General construction (residential, commercial)
Approx. Price (50kg): ₹350 – ₹420
Note: Price may vary by grade and region.


🧱 Portland Pozzolana Cement (PPC)
Composition: OPC + Fly ash
Uses: Dams, marine structures, sewage works
Approx. Price (50kg): ₹330 – ₹400
Note: Suitable for long-term durability and sustainability.


⚡ Rapid Hardening Cement
Property: Gains strength quickly
Uses: Precast works, road repairs
Approx. Price (50kg): ₹370 – ₹450
Note: Higher early strength compared to OPC.


🌊 Sulphate Resisting Cement
Property: Resists sulphate attack
Uses: Foundations in coastal/sewage environments
Approx. Price (50kg): ₹390 – ₹470
Note: Ideal for harsh chemical exposure.


❄️ Quick Setting Cement
Property: Sets fast
Uses: Underwater & cold weather construction
Approx. Price (50kg): ₹400 – ₹480
Note: Ideal where early setting is required.


🏗️ Low Heat Cement
Property: Reduces heat of hydration
Uses: Dams, mass concrete structures
Approx. Price (50kg): ₹420 – ₹500
Note: Prevents thermal cracking in large pours.


🔧 White Cement
Property: White finish
Uses: Decorative works, flooring, wall putty
Approx. Price (50kg): ₹900 – ₹1,100
Note: More expensive than OPC due to manufacturing process.


🎨 Colored Cement
Property: Pigmented finish
Uses: Tiles, paths, murals
Approx. Price (50kg): ₹950 – ₹1,200
Note: Made by adding pigments to white cement.


💧 Hydrophobic Cement
Property: Moisture resistant
Uses: High rainfall areas, long-term storage
Approx. Price (50kg): ₹360 – ₹450
Note: Water-repelling additives improve shelf life.
