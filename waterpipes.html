🚿 PVC Pipes (Polyvinyl Chloride)
Use: Cold water plumbing, irrigation
Features: Lightweight, affordable, non-corrosive
Approx. Price (1 inch, per 3m): ₹120 – ₹200
Note: Not suitable for hot water.
💧 CPVC Pipes (Chlorinated PVC)
Use: Hot & cold water distribution
Features: Heat-resistant, corrosion-free
Approx. Price (1 inch, per 3m): ₹250 – ₹350
Note: Ideal for residential plumbing.
🏡 UPVC Pipes (Unplasticized PVC)
Use: Water supply and drainage
Features: Rigid, chemical-resistant, lead-free
Approx. Price (1 inch, per 3m): ₹150 – ₹220
Note: Common in homes and apartments.
🏭 HDPE Pipes (High-Density Polyethylene)
Use: Agriculture, municipal water supply
Features: Flexible, durable, leak-proof
Approx. Price (1 inch, per meter): ₹35 – ₹55
Note: Good for long-distance underground piping.
🧰 PPR Pipes (Polypropylene Random Copolymer)
Use: Hot & cold water systems
Features: High pressure resistance, thermal insulation
Approx. Price (1 inch, per meter): ₹40 – ₹60
Note: Popular in industrial and commercial setups.
