⚡ FR (Flame Retardant) Wires
Use: Residential and commercial wiring
Features: Resists spreading of fire, good insulation
Approx. Price (90m coil): ₹1,200 – ₹1,600
Note: Most commonly used for indoor applications.


🔥 FRLS (Flame Retardant Low Smoke) Wires
Use: Enclosed public areas (hospitals, theaters)
Features: Emits low smoke, fire retardant
Approx. Price (90m coil): ₹1,600 – ₹2,000
Note: Safer in case of fire.


🏢 HRFR (Heat Resistant Flame Retardant) Wires
Use: High-temperature zones
Features: Withstands high heat and fire
Approx. Price (90m coil): ₹1,800 – ₹2,200
Note: Ideal for heavy electrical loads.


🛠️ Multi-Strand Copper Wires
Use: Household and industrial wiring
Features: Flexible, durable, good conductivity
Approx. Price (90m coil): ₹1,400 – ₹1,800
Note: Made of multiple strands for flexibility.


🔌 Coaxial Cable
Use: TV, CCTV, antenna connections
Features: Shielded signal transmission
Approx. Price (per meter): ₹15 – ₹30
Note: Designed for low signal loss.


🌐 LAN Cable (Cat 6)
Use: Internet and network connections
Features: High-speed data transfer
Approx. Price (per meter): ₹20 – ₹40
Note: Common in offices and smart homes.
